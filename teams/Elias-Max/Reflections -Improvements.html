Bridge 1

Heres What we Learned

Our bridge only held 1 pound, this is because each spaghetti beam was only one piece of spaghetti, rather than 2 or 3.

Even though, we got second place in the contest. The reason for this is because our bridge was SUPER light, it was 26 grams, so our score multiplier is off the charts.

Heres How we Can Improve

Now, we are going to make our spaghetti beams thicker, with 3-4 sticks per beam.

Our bridge is going to be a bit heavier, and much taller.

heres How we Can Improve
